Lead: 
Over the past years, I have worked within a broad variety of domains. Looking back, my contributions to different efforts to advance the current state of the art in research and practice can be clustered into the following four emerging topics: Smart Cities, Personal Finance, Open Research, and Interactive Storytelling.
